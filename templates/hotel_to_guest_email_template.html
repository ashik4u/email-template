Safety and Security Instructions

All travelers must follow the instructions outlined below while traveling to Bangladesh.

Do’s and Don’ts
  1. Travelers shall inform the Global Mobility team immediately if they face any issues at airport immigration. Contact: AKM Redhwan Sarwar at +8801914208140 or +8809610974000.
  2. Travelers should limit and avoid non-essential intra-city or inter-district travel.
  3. All travelers should be accompanied by a local staff member from the concerned business team during any movement for client/business meetings. If a local staff member is not available from the concerned business team, the local Markets team should accompany the traveler.
  4. It is advisable that all movements be completed during normal business hours only.
  5. Travelers must always carry their PwC ID card while traveling on business.
  6. Travelers must not stay in non-PwC-approved properties. If travel/stay arrangements are made by the client, these should be documented during the OCTA approval process, and the property details must be shared with the local Business Services team for their review and approval.
  7. Travelers should avoid using public transportation during the trip. They must use transportation from PwC-enlisted cab service providers only, arranged via the local Business Services team.
  8. Travelers must inform the local Business Services team immediately if there is any sudden change in the travel plan. The local Business Services team will assess the situation and guide the traveler accordingly.
  9. Staff are requested to adhere to all safety and security directives issued by the firm/Business Services team to avoid any contingencies.
  10. All travelers shall maintain awareness of local news and be aware of their surroundings at all times.
  11. All travelers shall not attempt to cross any roadblocks or any congregation of people.
  12. All travelers shall not attempt to intervene in any verbal or physical confrontations, or film or photograph disturbances they may come across.
  13. It is recommended to purchase a local mobile SIM upon arrival at the airport for ease of communication and to share the number with the local Business Services POC.
  14. Travelers are requested to check the International SOS Portal, which is accessible to all PwC staff, to consult security and medical information per country.
  15. For any assistance, travelers should reach out to the local Business Services POC immediately: A S M Rifter Hussain at +8801718136902 or Ashikul Islam at +8801673534756 or +8809610974000.
